Cajun Chicken Pasta

Description

Erin's favorite.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breast halves, cut into thin strips
  • 4 ounces linguine, cooked al dente
  • 2 teaspoons cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 thinly sliced green onion
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tbs chopped sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp dried basil
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Place chicken and Cajun seasoning in a bowl and toss to coat.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, saut√© chicken in butter or margarine until chicken is tender, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat add green onion, heavy cream, tomatoes, basil, salt, garlic powder, black pepper and heat through.
  4. Pour over hot linguine and toss with Parmesan cheese.
